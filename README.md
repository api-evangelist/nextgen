# NextGen Healthcare

NextGen Healthcare is a leading ambulatory care EHR and practice management platform providing REST and FHIR APIs for clinical data, scheduling, patient engagement, and healthcare interoperability. The platform offers Enterprise APIs comprising 400+ JSON-based RESTful routes covering EHR, practice management, and clinical workflows, as well as FHIR R4 Patient Access APIs, SMART on FHIR App Launch APIs, and Bulk FHIR APIs compliant with 21st Century Cures Act requirements.

## APIs

- **NextGen Enterprise API** - 400+ RESTful routes for EHR, PM, clinical, and administrative workflows
- **NextGen Enterprise Patient Access FHIR API** - FHIR DSTU2/R4 patient health record access
- **NextGen Office Patient FHIR API** - FHIR R4 patient access for NextGen Office practices
- **NextGen Office SMART App Launch FHIR API** - SMART on FHIR single-patient data access
- **NextGen Office Bulk FHIR API** - Multi-patient USCDI v1 bulk data export
- **NextGen Mirth Connect Integration Engine API** - HL7/FHIR interoperability engine
